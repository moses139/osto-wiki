Part 1 — Starter Gear (Dreamkeeper)

Inception Tokens

After reaching level 60 you will receive a Parcel Post containing materials used for early endgame progression.

Your endgame PvE journey begins with Inception Tokens. These allow you to purchase your first PvE gear and other essential progression items from the Inception Shop.

What to Buy First

  1. Dreamkeeper Set – Your first PvE gear set. Buy this first.
  2. Glyph Tokens – Used to unlock glyphs that enhance your class abilities.

Farming Inception Tokens

Once you purchase your first Dreamkeeper piece, you will need more Inception Tokens (or gear drops) to complete the full set. Both can be acquired from Kelsaik's Nest (Normal).

Tip: Weapons > Gloves > Chest > Boots. Since Weapon and Gloves contribute to damage output whereas Chest and Boots provide defense, it's typically best to prioritize obtaining/enchanting your Weapon first, then Gloves, then Chest, then Boots.

Part 7 — BiS Gear (Visionmaker)

The Visionmaker Set represents OSTO's Best-in-Slot (BiS) equipment for this progression tier. Unlike most endgame gear, Visionmaker gear is not obtained simply from dungeon drops and must instead be crafted.

Crafting Visionmaker requires collecting a wide variety of materials from across the game, including high-level PvE dungeons, reputation dailies, PvP rewards, and rare gathering resources. As a result, completing a full Visionmaker set typically requires participation in several different endgame activities. Players must also reach Artisan-level crafting in the appropriate profession before they can craft Visionmaker weapons/armor.

Glossary

Tokens

Inception Token – Exchange for Dreamkeeper Set
Glyph Token – Used to unlock or upgrade class glyphs that enhance abilities
Lesser Abyssal Mark – Exchange for Abyss Gloves and Boots
Greater Abyssal Mark – Exchange for Abyss Weapon and Chest
Mark of the Phoenix – Exchange for Mayhem Chest or Pristine Phoenix Beaks needed to craft Visionmaker Set

Gear Sets

Dreamkeeper Set – Tier 9, entry-level gear set
Abyss Set – Tier 10, mid-level gear set
Mayhem Set/Chest – Tier 10, High-level gear set (Chest piece only)
Regent Set – Tier 10, High-level gear set

Jewelry

Dangling Fork – Entry-level earring
Caged Raindrop – Mid-level earring
Phoenix Ear – High-end/BiS earring
Sunder Ring – Entry-level ring
Sprocket Ring – Mid-level ring
Phoenix Signet – High-end/BiS ring
Thulsan Curio – Entry-level necklace
Chain of Deceit – Mid-level necklace
Crown Jewels – High-end/BiS necklace

Enchanting Materials

Marvelous Alkahest – Enchanting material used to upgrade gear
Marvelous Feedstock – Enchanting material used to upgrade Tier 10 gear (e.g., Abyss, Mayhem, Regent, and Visionmaker)
Master Enigmatic Scroll – Converts unidentified gear into an enigmatic state so it can become masterworked or have its bonus stats rerolled
Intricate Identification Scroll – Identifies enigmatic gear, revealing the possibility of becoming masterworked and/or applying its randomized bonus stats
Tier 9 Feedstock – Base enchanting material used to upgrade Tier 9 gear (e.g., Dreamkeeper)

Visionmaker Crafting Materials

Visionmaker Designs – Crafting blueprints for Visionmaker Set
Dream Bindings – Material used to craft Visionmaker Armor
Fanesteel – Material used to craft Visionmaker Weapons
Pristine Phoenix Beak – Material used to craft Visionmaker Set
Kaelic Spark – Rare gathering catalyst used to craft Visionmaker equipment
Cyasmic Oil – Argon-derived oil used in Visionmaker crafting
Argon Vitriol – Hardened argon residue used to craft Visionmaker equipment
Chaos Block – Condensed nexus energy used in Visionmaker crafting
Victor’s Trophy – PvP token used to craft Visionmaker gear

Consumables

Nostrum of Energy – Consumable that temporarily boosts attack speed
Onslaught Scroll – Consumable that temporarily boosts a specific stat (e.g., attack speed, crit factor, crit power)
